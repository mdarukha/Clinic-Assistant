Our clinic is open from 8 AM to 6 PM, Monday through Friday. We are closed on weekends.

We are located at 456 Elm Street, Sacramento, CA.

We provide the following services:
- Primary care and wellness check-ups
- Physicals for student athletes and work/school requirements
- TB tests and clearance documentation
- Flu shots and COVID-19 vaccinations
- On-site lab testing
- X-rays and diagnostic imaging
- Chronic condition management (e.g., diabetes, hypertension)
- Injury evaluation and treatment (e.g., sprains, fractures, cuts, bruises)
- Women's health services (e.g., Pap smears, birth control)
- Mental health referrals and brief assessments

We serve patients of all ages, from children to seniors.

Our healthcare team includes licensed family physicians, nurse practitioners, and registered nurses.

We offer walk-in hours for urgent needs, though appointments are recommended.
